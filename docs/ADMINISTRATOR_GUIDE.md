# Administrator guide

1. Use an individual account and complete TOTP MFA.
2. Invite users from **Users & roles**; assign the least-privileged role.
3. Create each gathering under **Services** and assign the ushers who will check in visitors.
4. Review named attendance only when operationally necessary.
5. Correct an attendance record by providing a meaningful reason; never delete it informally.
6. Export personal data only for an approved purpose. Every export is logged.
7. Review audit events and disabled/inactive accounts regularly.
8. Apply retention only under the approved policy and after reviewing the preview count.
9. Immediately disable departed staff and remove unnecessary service assignments.
10. Report lost tablets, suspicious sign-ins, or unexpected exports through the incident procedure.
